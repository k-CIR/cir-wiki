# BIDSisifer
To promote the use of BIDS and simplify the implementation on the data on [SPICE](../../SPICE).

Once you have your files on the server, use the [NatMEG-BIDSifier](https://github.com/k-CIR/NatMEG-BIDSifier) to analyze, batch edit and organize your data in accordance with MEG-BIDS.
